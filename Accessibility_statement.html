Accessibility

Last updated: October 4, 2025

My Commitment

Mainstream Tinting is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities, in accordance with the principles of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).

1. Goal & Standards

I aspire to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA. I use high-contrast dark mode styling and semantic HTML to inherently promote usability.

2. Technical Status

I actively maintain the following:

  • Alt Text: Descriptive text for images and logos.
  • Keyboard Nav: Interactive elements are keyboard accessible.
  • Contrast: I utilize high-contrast text colors (light gray on dark backgrounds) for readability.

3. Feedback

If you encounter any barriers, please contact me. I prioritize accessibility fixes.
