Film Options

Solar Control Films

Ceramic, carbon, or metallic films that block 45-85% of solar heat gain. Reduce cooling costs by 15-30% while maintaining your view.

Clear UV Protection

Invisible film that blocks 99.9% of UV rays. Preserves furniture and flooring without changing appearance or natural light.

Decorative & Frosted

Instant privacy with frosted, etched, or custom patterns. 24/7 privacy while maintaining natural light diffusion.

Security & Impact

Thick films (4-14 mil) that hold broken glass together, preventing injury and delaying forced entry. Available in clear or tinted.
